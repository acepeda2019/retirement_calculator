4% Rule

The safe withdrawal strategy

Withdraw 4% of your starting balance every year. Your portfolio keeps growing — you'll likely leave a substantial estate. Designed to last 30+ years.

Fixed Drawdown

The spend-it-all strategy

Withdraw enough each year to reach $0 at your life expectancy. Since the remaining balance keeps compounding, withdrawals increase over time — more to spend as you age.
