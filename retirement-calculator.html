4% Rule

The safe withdrawal strategy

Withdraw 4% of your starting balance every year. Your remaining portfolio keeps growing, so you'll likely leave a substantial estate. Considered the "safe" rate designed to survive a 30-year retirement.

Fixed Drawdown

The spend-it-all strategy

Withdraw exactly enough each year to reach $0 at your life expectancy. Since the remaining balance keeps compounding, your annual withdrawals increase over time — giving you more to spend as you age.
